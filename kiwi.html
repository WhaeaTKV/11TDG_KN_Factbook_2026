Kiwi is from new Zealand Kiwi is a small, flightless bird native to New Zealand and a national symbol of the country. Kiwis have small bodies covered with brown or gray hair-like feathers and long beaks with nostrils at the tip, which they use to sniff out food at night. Kiwis are nocturnal and feed mainly on insects, worms, and other small invertebrates. They live in forests, shrublands, and grasslands and are usually solitary.
