New Zealand Falcon

This is a paragraph about the New Zealand Falcon and about it's attributes and population.

  • New Zealand Falcon is a magpie-sized raptor that feeds predominantly on live prey.
  • There are 3 ecological forms of New Zealand Falcon.Easter,Southern and Bush
  • A typical clutch consists of 5-6 eggs and takes about 60 days to hatch.
  • The population of New Zealand Falcon is between 5,000-8,000.
  • Adult New Zealand Falcons measure between 40cm and 50cm.
  • The New Zealand Falcon is capable of flying at speeds of up to 200km/h.
  • Male Falcons weigh between 240 g and 350g and females weigh between 410g and 720g.
